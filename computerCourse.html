Programming Course

No previous programming experience is required, but you must know decent english and how to use a computer. This course will teach you the fundamentals of some web-based programming languages in only 10 weeks.

Beginner Friendly.

Course Length: 10 Weeks.

Certificate of completion (Optional)..

Course Curriculam

  • 1st week HTML.
  • 2nd week CSS.
  • 3rd week Project with HTML and CSS.
  • 4th week JavaScript.
  • 5th week Project with JS.
  • 6-8 week PHP, MySql Databases, Bootstrap (optional).
  • 9th (Last week) Git and review.
  • 1 Day: Freelancing Class.
  • 1 Day: How to get a Job/Internship.

Note: Online & On campus classes both cost the same.

What’s the refund policy if I need to cancel?

All Admission fees are non-refundable. If you cannot attend your particular class, you must inform us no later than 7 days before your class starts, so we can allot that seat to someone else and move your date to a future batch. You are however allowed to send someone as a substitute if you wish, but you again must inform us atleast 3 days before the course starts. The above does not apply, if you unfortunately lose a family member. Talk to us when possible, and we’ll try our best to accommodate you accordingly.
